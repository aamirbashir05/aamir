Our Story

Wellness, wrapped in a bar of soap

The Healthy Dome began with a simple belief: your skin deserves ingredients as honest as the food you eat. What started in a home kitchen is now a growing line of cold-processed soaps, all-natural-oil perfumes and soothing wellness rubs.

Every product is handcrafted in small batches — no artificial colors, no synthetic fragrances, no harsh alcohols. Just plant oils, essential oils and real botanicals doing what nature intended.
